# PactPal — Legal

Public hosting for PactPal's **Privacy Policy** and **Terms of Service**, served via
GitHub Pages. Required by Google Play / the App Store (a policy at a public URL) and
linked from the store listings.

- Privacy Policy → `/privacy/`
- Terms of Service → `/terms/`

**Source of truth:** the canonical copy lives in the app repo at
`packages/core/src/shared/legal.ts` (rendered in-app) with long-form mirrors in
`docs/PRIVACY.md` / `docs/TERMS.md`. Keep this site in step when that content
changes, and bump the "Last updated" date.
