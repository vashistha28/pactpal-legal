Delete your account & data

Last updated 2026-07-03

You can permanently delete your PactPal account and personal data at any time. There are two ways — pick whichever suits you. Neither requires you to keep the app installed.

Option 1 — In the app (instant)

  • Open PactPal and go to Settings (the gear on the Circle tab).
  • Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account, then confirm.
  • Your account and data are removed immediately and you're signed out.

Option 2 — By email (no app needed)

If you no longer have the app installed, email us and we'll delete your account for you:

Request deletion by email →

Send it from (or tell us) the email address on your PactPal account so we can verify the request. We'll confirm once it's done, typically within a few days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile — name, email, and profile photo.
  • Your circle memberships.
  • Your check-ins (marks about you and by you), disputes, and votes.
  • Your device push tokens.

What may remain

  • Circles and pacts you created are not deleted so your circle can keep going, but they're detached from you (your authorship is cleared). Other members' own data stays theirs.
  • We keep no separate backups of your personal data beyond what routine, time-limited infrastructure backups may briefly retain before rotating out.

Deletion is permanent and can't be undone. Questions: gaurav.vashistha@gmail.com.
