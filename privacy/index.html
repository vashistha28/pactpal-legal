Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-07-03

PactPal is a small-group habit-accountability app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We keep it short because we collect little.

What we collect

  • Account details: your email, name, and (optionally) a profile photo you upload.
  • Circle activity: the pacts your circle creates, the check-ins you and your circle members record about each other (kept / missed, plus any short note), disputes, and votes.
  • Notifications: a device push token, so we can tell you when you were marked, when a vote needs you, or send a cheer.
  • Basic technical data needed to run the service (e.g. timestamps on the actions above).

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data. Ever.
  • We do not show ads and we do not share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not use your check-in data to build a profile of you beyond running the app.

How your data is used

  • To run the core loop: forming circles, letting members mark each other, streaks, disputes, and votes.
  • To send the notifications you enable (all are optional and can be turned off in Settings and your device).
  • Your name, photo, and check-in record are visible only to members of the circles you belong to — access is enforced by database security rules (row-level security), not just the app.

Where it's stored

Your data is stored with our backend provider (Supabase) on secure, access-controlled infrastructure. Access is restricted by row-level security so members only see their own circles.

Deleting your data

  • You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, memberships, check-ins, and push tokens.
  • You can also request deletion without the app (or by emailing us at the address below).

Children

PactPal is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). Please don't use it if you're under that age.

Changes & contact

If this policy changes materially we'll update the date above and note it in the app. Questions or deletion requests: gaurav.vashistha@gmail.com.
